<a name="Notes" id="Notes"></a><h2><span class="editsection">[<a href="/index.php?title=Healing_Signet&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a>]</span> <span class="mw-headline">Notes</span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:66px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Healing_Signet_Beta_Icon.jpg" class="image" title="Beta icon"><img alt="" src="/images/thumb/c/c8/Healing_Signet_Beta_Icon.jpg/64px-Healing_Signet_Beta_Icon.jpg" width="64" height="64" border="0" class="thumbimage" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Healing_Signet_Beta_Icon.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"><img src="/skins/common/images/magnify-clip.png" width="15" height="11" alt="" /></a></div>Beta icon</div></div></div>
<ul><li>"-40 armor" will result in taking double damage from skills that are affected by <a href="/wiki/Armor_rating" title="Armor rating">armor rating</a>.
</li><li><a href="/wiki/Hero" title="Hero">Heroes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Henchmen" title="Henchmen" class="mw-redirect">henchmen</a> with this skill will retreat from combat in order to use it.
</li><li>The "-40 armor" is factored in after the <a href="/wiki/Effect_stacking" title="Effect stacking">armor cap</a>. 
</li></ul>
<div style="margin: 1em; border: solid silver; border-width: 1px 0px; padding: 2px 5px 2px 5px;">
<div class="hiddenlinks" style="font-weight: bold; color: #900; float: left;"><a href="/wiki/File:Anomaly.png" class="image" title="Anomaly"><img alt="Anomaly" src="/images/thumb/a/a6/Anomaly.png/18px-Anomaly.png" width="18" height="18" border="0" /></a> <a href="/wiki/Anomaly" title="Anomaly" class="mw-redirect">Anomaly</a>.</div>
<div style="margin-left: 7.5em;">Despite the skill description, this skill heals rather than giving health gain.</div>
</div>

</li></ul>
